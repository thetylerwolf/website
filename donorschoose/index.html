School Poverty Level vs Value of Funding Requests on DonorsChoose.org

Number of funding requests categorized by poverty level over time from 2002 - 2016. There is a clear increase in requests year over year for all poverty levels. Highest poverty schools consistently request the most funding every year. Classroom supplies and technology are the most-cited resources needed across all poverty levels. (See: DonorsChoose.org poverty levels explained)

Drag the mouse over each year to see detailed statistics about each poverty level's projects.
